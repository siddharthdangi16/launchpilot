Refunds & cancellation policy

We want you to be confident in your decision. Our refund policy is designed to be fair and transparent.

  • Before program start: Full refund if you cancel more than 14 days before your cohort's start date
  • Within 14 days of first session: Full refund if requested within 14 days of attending your first live Sunday Session — no questions asked
  • After 14-day window: No refund is available once you have attended more than 2 live sessions or accessed more than 20% of the program materials
  • Deferral: You may defer to the next available cohort (same program) once at no charge, provided you notify us at least 7 days before your cohort starts
  • Program cancellation by LaunchPilot: If we cancel a cohort, you will receive a full refund or the option to transfer to the next cohort
  • Non-attendance: Failing to attend sessions does not constitute a request for refund. You must actively request a refund within the applicable window

To request a refund or deferral, email admissions@launchpilotschool.com with your full name, program, and cohort date.
