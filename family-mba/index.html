Who is it for

Built for the next
generation of legacy.

This program is not for everyone. It is designed for a very specific kind of leader — one who carries the weight of generations and the ambition to take it further.

01
Second & Third Generation Inheritors
You've taken over a business built by your parents or grandparents. You want to modernise it, scale it with AI, and put your own stamp on its next chapter — without breaking what your family built.
02
Family Enterprise Professionals
You work within the family business — as a director, operations head, or business unit leader. You want the MBA credential, the strategic tools, and the peer network to lead at a higher level.
03
Promoters Building for the Next 100 Years
You're already in the driver's seat — but you see disruption coming. AI, globalisation, shifting consumer behaviour. This program gives you the toolkit to adapt, diversify, and lead your family business into the next era.
