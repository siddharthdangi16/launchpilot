Curriculum

6 modules.
One real startup.

Every module is 6 weeks. Every module ends with a real deliverable — not an exam. You graduate with a company, a financial model, and your first customers.

Module 01 Design Thinking & Problem Discovery ▾
Customer obsession & first-principles thinking — understand your user at a cellular level
Problem validation: Jobs-To-Be-Done, ICP mapping, full user journey design
Market sizing — TAM, SAM, SOM with real data, not guesswork
Rapid validation using AI: GPT workflows, user research automation
Lean experimentation, hypothesis testing, and kill criteria
Bootcamp: Design Thinking Onboarding — Deep empathy building, idea generation, prototype & iterate
Module Deliverable
Validate a startup problem with real user insights, market data, and a tested hypothesis
Module 02 Product & MVP Development ▾
MVP thinking — speed over perfection, ship and learn in days not months
No-code / low-code tools + AI-assisted product building (Bubble, Webflow, Glide)
Agentic AI development — build AI agents and growth-on-autopilot systems
Product roadmapping, RICE/ICE prioritization, backlog management
UX/UI fundamentals for founders — what makes a product people love
Building AI-enabled products: wrappers, automation tools, AI copilots
Bootcamp: Agentic AI & No-Code Development — Build your MVP in a weekend
Module Deliverable
Launch your MVP — a live product or working prototype with your first 10 users
Module 03 Sales & Customer Acquisition ▾
Founder-led sales — how to sell when you are the product
B2B vs B2C strategies, pipeline building, and CRM systems
Cold outreach mastery — email, LinkedIn, warm intros, follow-up systems
Sales psychology — buying decisions, objection handling, closing without pressure
AI for sales automation: lead generation, outreach sequencing, reporting
Build repeatable revenue engines — from first 10 customers to 100+
Bootcamp: Sales Psychology — Pitch to an Investor Like a Pro
Module Deliverable
Acquire your first paying customers — a documented, repeatable sales process
Module 04 Marketing, Brand & Growth ▾
Growth loops & distribution strategy — find your unfair growth channel
Personal branding & founder storytelling — Speak & Influence at scale
Performance marketing: Meta Ads, Google Ads, SEO fundamentals
Content engines — LinkedIn, Twitter/X, short-form video, newsletter flywheels
AI-driven growth: content generation, audience targeting, analytics, experimentation
Go-to-market strategy — positioning, messaging, launch playbooks
Bootcamp: Speak & Influence — Craft compelling narratives that convert
Module Deliverable
A complete go-to-market strategy with measurable growth metrics and a live campaign
Module 05 Finance, Fundraising & Business Models ▾
Capital & Financial Intelligence — accounting, core finance, corporate valuation
Financial modelling and management decision-making frameworks
Unit economics & pricing — CAC, LTV, payback periods, margins
Fundraising strategy — VCs, angels, grants, and alternative funding
Investor pitch deck construction and storytelling that converts
Term sheets, cap tables, dilution — navigate deals confidently
Bootcamp: Capital & Financial Intelligence — Model, pitch, and raise
Module Deliverable
Complete financial model + investor-ready pitch deck for Demo Day
Module 06 Leadership, Scaling & Founder Mindset ▾
Founder psychology, resilience frameworks, and anti-burnout systems
Hiring your first 5 people — what to look for, where to find them
Culture & org design from day one — values, rituals, operating norms
Decision-making under uncertainty — frameworks used by top operators
Scaling operations & systems — from chaos to repeatable processes
2 Capstone projects — your company roadmap for the next 18 months
Capstone: 18-month roadmap + Live Demo Day with investors & ecosystem partners
Module Deliverable
Hiring plan, org structure, 18-month roadmap + live investor Demo Day
